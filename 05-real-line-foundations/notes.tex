\subsubsection*{Structural Roadmap}

The development of sequence theory in this chapter follows a
definition–theorem–structure architecture.

Each major topic is organized as:

\begin{center}
\textbf{Definitions $\longrightarrow$ Main Theorems $\longrightarrow$ Consequences and Logical Structure}
\end{center}

The global progression is:

\begin{enumerate}
\item Foundations: sequences and convergence
\item Compactness through subsequences
\item Completeness via Cauchy theory
\item Order structure and monotonicity
\item Extremal limiting behavior
\item Growth and asymptotics
\item Infinite series
\item Multi-index limits
\item Applications
\end{enumerate}
