\textbf{Goal.}
Prove the following identity for all $a, b \in \mathbb{N}$:
\[
(a + b)^2 \;=\; a^2 + 2ab + b^2.
\]
Here exponentiation is as defined in Definition~2.3.11, and $2ab$ denotes
$ab + ab$. You may use all results from \S2.2 and \S2.3 without further
justification.
