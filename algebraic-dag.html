IS-A Strict extension — child satisfies all parent axioms plus at least one new constraint. Every instance of the child IS an instance of the parent.
HAS-A Composition — the structure contains or is built from another structure as a component. Ring HAS-A Abelian Group (under +) and HAS-A Monoid (under ·).
USES-A External dependency — one structure acts on or requires another without inheriting from it. Vector Space USES-A Field for its scalar domain.
