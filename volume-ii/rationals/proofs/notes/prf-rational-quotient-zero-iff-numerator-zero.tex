\begin{theorem*}[A Rational Quotient Is Zero Exactly When Its Numerator Is Zero]
For every $a,b\in\mathbb{Q}$ with $b\neq 0$,
\[
\frac{a}{b}=0
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
a=0.
\]
\end{theorem*}
