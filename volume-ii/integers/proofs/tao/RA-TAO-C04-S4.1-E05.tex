\noindent\textbf{Goal.}
Prove Proposition~4.1.8: if $a, b \in \mathbb{Z}$ and $ab = 0$,
then $a = 0$ or $b = 0$.

\vspace{0.75em}

\noindent\textbf{Logical form.}
$\forall a, b \in \mathbb{Z}:\; ab = 0 \Rightarrow (a = 0 \vee b = 0)$.

Definition~4.1.2 (multiplication formula),
