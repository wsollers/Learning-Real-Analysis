\section{Induction}

This section should gather the main induction principles on $\mathbb{N}$:
ordinary induction, strong induction, induction from an arbitrary starting
point, and the equivalence of the usual formulations used later in the book.

\begin{remark}[Tooling layer]
Induction is not just another theorem in the chapter. It is part of the
chapter's proof infrastructure and should be presented as such.
\end{remark}
