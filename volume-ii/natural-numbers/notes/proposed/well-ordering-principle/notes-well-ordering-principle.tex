\section{Well-Ordering Principle}

This section should isolate least-element reasoning on $\mathbb{N}$ and make
the equivalence between induction and well-ordering explicit. It is also the
natural home for least counterexample and first crossing arguments.

\begin{remark}[Why this is separate]
Well-ordering is closely related to induction, but it supports a distinct
style of reasoning that deserves its own local toolkit.
\end{remark}
