\section{Addition on \texorpdfstring{$\mathbb{N}$}{N}}

This section should define addition recursively and then develop its first
consequences: addition by zero, addition by successor, successor as adding
one, cancellation, associativity, commutativity, and the additive identity.

\begin{remark}[Local focus]
This is the additive lawbook for $\mathbb{N}$. It should stay close to the
recursive definition and build the arithmetic from there.
\end{remark}
