\textbf{Goal.}
Prove Proposition~2.3.6 (Multiplication preserves order): if $a, b, c$
are natural numbers with $a < b$ and $c > 0$, then $ac < bc$.

\medskip
\noindent
\textbf{Logical form.}
$\forall\, a, b, c \in \mathbb{N},\quad a < b \text{ and } c > 0
\;\Rightarrow\; ac < bc.$
