\noindent\textbf{Problem (Abbott 1.3.4).}
Let $A_1,A_2,A_3,\dots$ be a collection of nonempty sets, each of which is bounded above.
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]
\item Find a formula for $\sup(A_1\cup A_2)$.
\item Extend this formula to $\sup\!\left(\bigcup_{k=1}^{n}A_k\right)$.
\item Does the formula in (b) extend to the infinite case
$\sup\!\left(\bigcup_{k=1}^{\infty}A_k\right)$?
\end{enumerate}
