\textbf{Claim.}
Let $A,B \subseteq \mathbb{R}$ and $C=A\cup B$. If $A$ and $B$ are bounded above, then
\[
\sup C = \max\{\sup A,\sup B\}.
\]

\textbf{Proof.}

\qed
