Let $\varepsilon > 0$. Since $x_n \to x$, there exists $N$ such that
$d(x_n,x)<\varepsilon/2$ for all $n\ge N$. If $m,n\ge N$, then
\[
d(x_m,x_n)\le d(x_m,x)+d(x,x_n)<\varepsilon.
\]
